What is the ZKP presale auction, how does it work, and how long does it last?

The Zero Knowledge Proof presale auction is a daily auction starting on 27 November at 03:00 UTC, with up to 200 million $ZKP available per day. The daily allocation reduces with each phase, and any unused supply is permanently burned.

The price is set by participation — the more demand in a given day, the higher the price. Everyone who contributes on the same day receives the same price.

35% of the total supply (90 billion $ZKP) is allocated to this presale auction.

It's designed to reward early participants and create fair price discovery without bots or gas wars.

What is a Proof Pod and what is the difference between compute and storage Pods?

A Proof Pod is a physical validator node — a plug-and-play device that contributes to the Zero Knowledge Proof network by completing compute or storage tasks. Users can earn rewards by keeping their Proof Pod connected and participating in the network. There are two main types of Pods:

  • Compute Pods execute zero-knowledge proofs and AI validation tasks — earning rewards based on task complexity and speed.
  • Storage Pods offer verifiable, off-chain storage — earning rewards based on data capacity and uptime.

Each serves a distinct but complementary function in the ZKP ecosystem. All Pods are powered and connected via the ZKP Dashboard.

What is the $ZKP Coin used for?

$ZKP Coin is the native coin of the Zero Knowledge Proof network. It is used for:

  • Staking to secure the network
  • Paying for computation and storage tasks
  • Incentivizing validators (via Proof Pods)
  • Accessing services like AI models, data markets, and smart contracts
  • Participating in governance, including proposal voting and protocol upgrades
What is the ZKP Dashboard and how do I access it — including from mobile?

The ZKP Dashboard is your control center for interacting with the Zero Knowledge Proof network. From the dashboard, you can:

  • Buy $ZKP Coin through the presale auction
  • Manage your Proof Pod(s)
  • Track your earnings and tasks
  • View contribution and coin allocation history
  • Access governance tools (when live)

To access the dashboard, visit buy.zkp.com from any desktop or mobile browser and connect your Web3 wallet.

The dashboard is fully mobile-friendly and works in any modern browser. There is no separate app, but you can manage your Proof Pod, contribute to auctions, and track balances directly from mobile.

What is the total supply of $ZKP coins?

How do I activate and link my Proof Pod to my ZKP account?

To activate a Proof Pod:

  1. Connect the USB-C power cable and turn on the device.
  2. Connect to Wi-Fi using the device screen.
  3. On another device, go to zkp.com, log in, and link your wallet.
  4. Enter the one-time code shown on the Proof Pod screen.
What is the ZKP vesting schedule?

$ZKP coins are released over five months, with 20% unlocking each month:

  2. Month 2: 20% unlocked

  4. Month 4: 20% unlocked
